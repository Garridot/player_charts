what is Football Player Charts?

Football Player Charts is an API where you can see how much a player influences his team (the percentage of the goals scored, his favorite victims, his performance by competition, and his percentage of goals and assists per game). You can also compare their stats to each other.

How the data is collected?

The data is collected and it's updated through a web scraping on transfermarkt.com.
